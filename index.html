Are you going to travel without internet connection? Is your internet connection too slow? Is your computer too slow since reading manga on the fly because of ads? Or just want to read Hentai somewhere your parent or girl friend cannot catch it?

So, this tiny tool was born to help you to get it. It aims to download manga on the internet to your computer to view them at the later time on your computers or phones. And certainly, it will always be 100% FREE.

Feel free to feedback to me any errors and new features you want via email dangduy2910@gmail.com or leave comments here. Thanks for using.

Some Features

  • Ability to download up to 10 manga simultaneously with one-click (Unlimited with Add to Queue)
  • Ability to download a manga/chapter from its link (Grabber tool)
  • Allow to create automatically a ZIP or PDF file from each chapter was downloaded completely
  • Advanced search across sites
  • Automatically update manga list. No longer worry about the manga list is out of date
  • Shutdown your computer after download queue was complete
  • Create a ZIP/PDF file from anything downloaded manually (Converter tool)

FAQs

  • 1. How to download the manga immediately?
    Choose manga/chapters you want to download (on the left/right list) -> right-click on the list -> Download.
    Remember that you can only download up to 10 manga at the same time.
  • 2. How to bookmark and download manga later or more than 10 manga at the same time?
    Choose manga/chapters you want to download (on the left/right list) -> right-click on the list -> Add to Queue. It should be shown in the bottom list.
    Remember that it just added to the list. To start downloading, click button Start Queue .
